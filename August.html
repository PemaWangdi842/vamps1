Upcoming Events (August)

SHERUBTSE’S SHARED SHELF

Mr. Chogyal Dorji, a third-year student from B.A. in History, initiated Sherubtse’s Shared Shelf in collaboration with the Happiness and Wellbeing Centre. This activity aims to promote reading habits and create a community of readers at Sherubtse College. It will also host book sessions and explore the possibility of organizing book festivals and advocacy programs in nearby schools.

The activity is organized on weekends every week.

MONSOON FOOTBALL TOURNAMENT

The most awaited Monsoon Football Tournament will begin from September 6, 2021. It is one of the most popular domestic tournaments held annually at Sherubtse College during the autumn semester. Due to the pandemic, the tournament could not be held in 2020. This year, the tournament will be played behind closed doors, with 22 teams, including a staff team, participating. The final match is scheduled for September 23, 2021.

KIDDIE DANCE CLASS

The Happiness and Well-being Centre, in collaboration with the Culture Club, will host a Kiddie Dance Class to reduce screen time for children aged 6-15. The program will encourage music and dance while promoting teamwork and building self-confidence.

BASIC COUNSELLING SKILLS’ COURSE

The Happiness and Well-being Centre will conduct a workshop on ‘Basic Counselling Skills’ from September 13 to October 18, 2021. The course will focus on self-discovery, communication skills, mindfulness practice, and basic counselling techniques. Registration is open from September 1 to September 8, 2021.
