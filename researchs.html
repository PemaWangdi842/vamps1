Research Facilities & Equipment

Our college is equipped with state-of-the-art research facilities and cutting-edge equipment to support a variety of research endeavors. Below is an overview of some of the key facilities available:

  • Science Laboratory

    Equipped with the latest instruments for conducting experiments in physics, chemistry, and biology.

  • Environmental Research Lab

    Facilities for studying environmental impacts, sustainability, and climate change-related research.

  • Archaeological Dig Site

    A dedicated space for excavation and preservation of historical artifacts, with advanced tools for archaeological research.

  • Data Science Lab

    A specialized facility for research involving big data, machine learning, and computational modeling.

Additionally, we have a variety of equipment, such as microscopes, computers, climate control systems, and software, to aid in the research process.

Research Works and Projects

Our college is engaged in a variety of research projects across multiple disciplines. Below are some of the ongoing and completed research works and projects:

  • Climate Change and Its Effects on Local Agriculture

    This project focuses on studying the impact of changing weather patterns on the farming community in our region.

  • Ancient Artifacts and Cultural Preservation

    An archaeological research project examining the preservation of ancient Bhutanese artifacts and their significance in history.

  • Population Growth and Urbanization

    This study looks into the effects of rapid population growth and urbanization on local ecosystems and infrastructure.

These projects are funded through our college’s annual research grants, as well as international collaborations with other universities.
