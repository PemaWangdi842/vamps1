Digital Resources

Explore a world of knowledge through Sherubtse College's curated selection of digital resources.

ProQuest Ebook Central

Sherubtse College has selected ProQuest Ebook Central as a key ebook provider. ProQuest is committed to empowering researchers and librarians worldwide. Its innovative information content and technologies increase the productivity of students, scholars, professionals, and the libraries that serve them.

Through partnerships with content holders, ProQuest preserves rich, vast, and varied information—whether historical archives or today’s scientific breakthroughs—and packages it with digital technologies that enhance discovery, sharing, and management. For academic, corporate, government, school, and public libraries, ProQuest provides services that enable strategic acquisition, management, and discovery of information collections.

Learn More about ProQuest

JSTOR

Sherubtse has subscribed to JSTOR, which provides access to more than 12 million academic journal articles, books, and primary sources across 75 disciplines. JSTOR is an invaluable resource for students, researchers, and educators looking to access a diverse collection of scholarly materials.

Explore JSTOR

EBSCOhost

EBSCOhost provides users with the ability to search, discover, and access a vast variety of content, including:

  • Millions of pages of archives
  • Hundreds of full-text and secondary databases
  • Hundreds of thousands of eBook and Audiobook titles

EBSCOhost is an essential tool for accessing a comprehensive range of academic materials to support research and learning.
