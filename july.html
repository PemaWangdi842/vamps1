What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood through adulthood.

Many factors contribute to mental health problems, including:

  • Biological factors, such as genes
  • Life experiences, such as trauma or abuse
  • Family history of mental health problems

At Sherubtse, mental health-related services are available at any hour of the day. Anyone wishing to use these services may visit the Happiness and Well-being Center during office hours or call us toll-free at #6006 anytime.

Note: If treatment is delayed, it can cause severe damage to one's mental health.
