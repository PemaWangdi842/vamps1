Bachelor of Economics & Political Science

The Bachelor of Economics and Political Science provides a general platform for students to create and contribute to knowledge generation in an era driven by technology, digitization, and an ever-changing multicultural economy. This program equips students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles and concepts of both fields, enabling them to analyse complex economic and political issues and develop solutions using econometric and policy analysis tools.

Program Highlights

This dual-disciplinary program combines the study of economics and political science, giving students insight into how economic theories and political systems interact to shape global and local contexts. Students gain:

  • Knowledge of economic theories and their real-world applications
  • Insight into political systems, institutions, and processes
  • Critical thinking and analytical skills
  • Skills in effective communication and a global perspective

Graduates are prepared for careers in government, business, international organizations, academia, and more. The program also instills a commitment to ethical behavior, social responsibility, and civic engagement, preparing students to make positive contributions in their communities and the world.
