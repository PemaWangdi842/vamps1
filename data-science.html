Bachelor of Data Science & Data Analytics

The Bachelor of Data Science and Data Analytics programme is designed to equip students with adaptive skills to manage technological disruptions and seize emerging opportunities in the evolving job market. The curriculum integrates Data Science, Statistics, and Mathematics to train students in data handling, visualization, and analytics to solve complex real-world problems.

Students will gain hands-on experience with data and learn to derive actionable insights that can aid strategic decision-making for leaders and managers.

Program Highlights

The program focuses on building core data competencies, enabling students to perform data wrangling, analyze data, and convert it into intelligent insights. Graduates will be prepared to apply analytical and technical skills across various domains, driving informed decision-making processes.

Yearly Modules

Year Modules
Year I Introduction to Data Science, Basic Statistics, Foundations of Mathematics, Programming Fundamentals, Data Visualization
Year II Intermediate Statistics, Data Handling Techniques, Database Management, Linear Algebra, Data Ethics and Privacy
Year III Advanced Statistical Methods, Machine Learning, Data Mining, Time Series Analysis, Research Methodologies
Year IV Capstone Project, Big Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, Advanced Data Visualization, Internship
